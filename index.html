My Stance

AI doesn't replace the writer. It raises the bar for what writing means. The craft now includes knowing what to prompt, what to rewrite, and what to protect from automation entirely.

I work with AI as a thinking partner. Drafting, stress-testing, iterating. But human judgment stays at the center of every content decision.

The result: copy that's faster to ship, harder to improve, and impossible to mistake for generic output.

Annika specializes in the tricky stuff: AI product copy, conversation design, and the microcopy that sits between a user's intent and a system's response. She's particularly good at making complex features feel simple and trustworthy.

About

Annika Bhargava

UX Writer and Content Designer with 8+ years shaping how products talk to people. I've spent my career at the intersection of language and technology, writing the words that help people trust, understand, and actually use the products they interact with every day.

I believe the best UX writing is invisible. You don't notice it. You just know what to do next. And in the age of AI, that clarity matters more than ever.

I've worked across consulting, edtech, and enterprise software. From rewriting checkout flows at Capgemini to building content systems at Microsoft. These days I'm focused on making AI products feel less like black boxes and more like helpful tools you can actually rely on.
